1. What is a Program?

A program is a set of instructions written in a programming language to perform a specific task on a 
computer or mobile device. For example, in Paytm, when we enter an amount and click on “Pay,” the 
program checks the balance, verifies details, and completes the payment.

4. Algorithm: Ordering Food on Swiggy

1. Open the Swiggy app.
2. Enter or select your delivery location.
3. Search for a restaurant or food item.
4. Select the food item you want to order.
5. Add the item to the cart.
6. Check the total amount and delivery address.
7. Apply coupon if available.
8. Choose a payment method.
9. Complete the payment.
10. Receive order confirmation.
