		Comparison of Databases


	1.MySQL
	Used for web applications.
	Fast and open-source.
	Example: Websites, e-commerce.


  	2. PostgreSQL
	Used for large web apps and data analytics.
	Advanced and highly reliable.
	Example: Business applications.


		3. Oracle
	Used for enterprise systems.
	High security and performance.
	Example: Banks, hospitals, government.
   


	4.SQLite
	Used for mobile and desktop apps.
	Lightweight and serverless.
	Example: Android apps, small projects.
